Shihao Zhou (周世豪)

I am a first year PhD student at Nankai University where I am advised by Prof. Jufeng Yang. Before that, I completed my Master at Xiamen University with my advisor Prof. Yunqi Lei and obtained my BS degree at Zhejiang Gongshang University. During my master, I was interested in 3D Computer Vision and more specifically on 3D human pose estimation.
